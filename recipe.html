Popular Recipes for Appetizer


Meatballs


Ingredients

4 dozen frozen meatballs 1 tablespoon dried minced onion 1 cup currant jelly 1 1/2 cups barbecue sauce 1/2 cup thinly sliced green onion, optional 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, optional.

Step by step

1. Put the frozen meatballs into a slow cooker.
2. In a bowl, mix the minced onion, currant jelly, and barbecue sauce.
3.Pour the sauce over the meatballs and stir to coat.
4. Cover and cook:
LOW: 3–4 hours
HIGH: 1½–2 hours
5. Serve hot. Garnish with green onions or cilantro if you like.



Finger-Sandwiches


ingredients

4 tablespoons cream cheese (softened) 4 teaspoons mustard 6 slices bread 6 cheddar cheese 8 slices deli ham baby lettuce leaves.

Step by step

1. Spread cream cheese and mustard on 3 slices of bread.
2. On one slice, add cheese, ham, and lettuce.
3. Top with the second slice of bread, then repeat: cheese, ham, lettuce.
4. Finish with the last slice of bread.
5. Cut the sandwich in half.
6. Stick toothpicks evenly into each half, then cut into 12 small pieces.
7. Repeat with remaining ingredients.

Pasta


Ingredients

½ lb (8 oz) spaghetti or pasta of choice 2 tsp olive oil 4 cloves garlic, minced 2-4 tbsp butter 3 cups chicken broth ¾ cup heavy cream 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 ½ tbsp dried parsley (or fresh) Salt and black pepper to taste Optional: 1 pinch cayenne pepper.

Step by step

1. Heat the oven to 400°F.
2. Toss cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Put them in a baking dish and place the feta in the center. Add a little pepper.
3. Bake 30 minutes, then increase oven to 450°F and bake 10–15 more minutes until tomatoes burst and everything is golden.
4. While that bakes, boil salted water and cook pasta until al dente. Save ½ cup pasta water, then drain.
5. Stir garlic and red pepper flakes into the hot tomatoes and feta. Mash into a creamy sauce.
6. dd pasta and half the basil; toss to coat. Add pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
7. Season to taste and serve with remaining basil and a pinch of sea salt.



Roast Chicken


Ingredients

Whole Chicken: 3.5 to 5-pound roasting chicken Butter/Oil: 3–4 tablespoons butter (melted) or olive oil Seasoning: 1 tablespoon kosher salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper, 1 teaspoon garlic powder or onion powder Aromatics: 1 lemon (halved), 1 head of garlic (cut in half crosswise), fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary, or sage) Optional Bedding: 2-3 carrots and 1 onion, roughly chopped.


Step by step

1. Preheat oven to 400°F for convection bake or 425°F if using a standard oven.
2. Zest 2 of the lemons. Strip the leaves off of 1 of the rosemary sprigs and chop it up finely.
3. In a bowl, combine the softened butter, lemon zest, rosemary, and salt and pepper to taste.
4. Line a baking sheet with heavy duty aluminum foil and lay the patted-dry chicken on the foil, breast-side up.
5. Use your fingers to smear the butter mixture all over the chicken, under the skin, and inside the cavity. Cut the lemons in half and squeeze the juice of 1 lemon all over the chicken. Place the 6 lemon halves (if they'll fit!) and the 3 remaining rosemary leaves in the cavity of the bird.
6. Place the chicken in the oven and roast it for 1 hour, 15 minutes or until done. The skin should be deep golden brown and juices should be sizzling.
7. Carve/cut the chicken to your heart's content, and dig in!

Popular Recipes for Dessert


Caramel-Topped Ice Cream


Ingredients

2 cups (about 16) finely crushed shortbread cookies 1/4 cup cashews, finely chopped 1/3 cup Land O Lakes Butter, melted 1 half-gallon vanilla ice cream, softened 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar 1/2 cup Land O Lakes Butter 2 (5-ounce) cans evaporated milk 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 cup pretzel sticks, coarsely crushed 1/2 cup cashews, coarsely chopped 1 (12-ounce) jar caramel ice cream topping, warmed.


Step by step

1. Mix crust ingredients and press into a 13x9 pan. Freeze 10 minutes.
2. Spread ice cream over crust. Freeze 1–2 hours.
3. Cook fudge ingredients (except vanilla) until boiling (7–9 min).
4. Reduce heat and cook 5 min more. Add vanilla and cool completely.
5. Pour fudge over ice cream. Freeze 4 hours or overnight.
6. Mix pretzels and cashews.
7. Let dessert sit 10–15 min. Cut and top with pretzel mix and caramel.


Fudgy Chocolate Squares


Ingredients

Cooking spray 1 (9-ounce; 255 g) package chocolate wafer cookies (about 72 cookies), such as Dewey’s, Oreos, or Goya Chocolate Maria Cookies (see notes) 8 ounces unsalted butter (226 g; 2 sticks), cut into 1 tablespoon pieces, divided 1/2 ounce granulated sugar (13 g; 1 tablespoon) 3/4 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt, divided; for table salt, use half as much by volume 12 ounces chopped bittersweet chocolate (340 g; 2 1/2 cups), 70% cacao, divided 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk 2 teaspoons instant espresso powder 2 teaspoons vanilla extract Flaky sea salt, to garnish.


Step by step

1. Spray an 8×8 pan with cooking spray. Line with parchment, leaving 2-inch overhang. Spray parchment.
2. Pulse cookies in a food processor until fine crumbs.
3. Melt 8 tbsp butter in microwave. Mix with crumbs, sugar, and 1/4 tsp salt.
4. Press crumb mixture into pan and freeze 15 minutes.
5. Melt 2 cups chocolate + 4 tbsp butter in microwave.
6. Stir in sweetened condensed milk, espresso, vanilla, and 1/2 tsp salt.
7. Pour over crust and refrigerate 1 hour.
8. Melt remaining 1/2 cup chocolate + 4 tbsp butter.
9. Pour over chilled layer and refrigerate 30 minutes.
10. Sprinkle sea salt. Lift out bars and cut into 36 squares.
